Welcome to passGen V 1.0

Generate a random password with the desired length (from 4 to 50 characters)

The password will contain the following:

  • at least one uppercase letter ( A - Z )
  • at least one lowercase letter ( a - z )
  • at least one digit [ 0 - 9 ]
  • at least one of the symbols: $ ! # @ % ^ & * ( )
